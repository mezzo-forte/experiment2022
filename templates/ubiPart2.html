Now that you now know your intial endowment (inherited income) that was assigned to you at the beginning of the previous stage, your income composition slightly changes (the gross income would be now displayed inclusive of your endowment).
However, the task rules remain the same.

► You are a member of the society of {{ group_size }} individuals where everyone is engaged in three types of work: paid-work, unpaid-work or no work. Paid work generates income.
► Each member in the group knows about their randomly assigned level of initial endowment ranging anywhere between € {{ lower_range }} to € {{ upper_range }}.
► All members of the society receive an unconditional transfer (G) of € {{ amount_UBI }} each month from the state without any exceptions and regardless of their involvement in paid-work.
► If you take up paid-work, you receive an income (in addition to € {{ amount_UBI }}) according to tasks completed (h) and constant wage rate (w) of € {{ wage_rate }} per month.
► You have 1 month of possible working time (1 month = 1 task, 12 tasks = 1 year). You can decide how to spend this time between available work types.
