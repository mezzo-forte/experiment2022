General Description of the Experiment


► The experiment consists of five parts:
1) First part consists of voting (in synchrony with other participants in the group) on voting principle that will take approximately 4 minutes.
2) Second part consists of voting (in synchrony with other participants in the group) on taxation system that will take approximately 1 minute.
3) Third part - first stage consists of 12 tasks that will take approximately 4-5 minutes and it will be played individually.
3) Third part - second stage consists of 12 tasks that will take approximately 4-5 minutes approximately and it will be played individually.
4) Fourth part consists of selecting your choice on taxation system individually that will take approximately 1 minute.
5) Fifth part consists of a questionnaire (to be answered individually) that will take approximately 4-6 minutes.



► The currency used in the experiment is EUR
